// Our Journey */

From humble beginnings to tech excellence

2020 - Foundation

Coding Club PRPCEM was founded with a vision to create a vibrant tech community. Started with 25 enthusiastic members and a passion for coding.

2021 - First Hackathon

Organized our first major hackathon with 100+ participants from various colleges. This marked the beginning of our journey in competitive programming.

2022 - Recognition

Received recognition as the "Best Student Tech Community" in the region. Our members won multiple national-level competitions.

2023 - Global Reach

Expanded our reach with international collaborations. Launched online workshops and connected with tech communities worldwide.

2024 - Innovation Hub

Established as a premier innovation hub with 500+ active members, multiple successful startups, and industry partnerships.

2025 - Future Forward

Continuing our mission to push boundaries in technology education, AI innovation, and building the next generation of tech leaders.

About Coding Club PRPCEM

Who We Are

Welcome to the Coding Club at PRPCEM! We are a vibrant community dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration in technology. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, our club provides a platform to explore, learn, and excel.

Through hackathons, workshops, and regular coding sessions, we aim to empower students to make meaningful contributions to the tech world.

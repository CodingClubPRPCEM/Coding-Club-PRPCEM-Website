About the Hackathon


The PRPCEM Hackathon 2025 is an exciting event where participants will engage in coding challenges, collaborate with peers, and innovate solutions to real-world problems.

Welcome to PRPCEM HACKATHON 2025

Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with learning, creativity, and fun. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there's something for everyone!

19th Dec 2024 - 13th Jan 2025

Joining the WhatsApp Group: Registered participants will be added to the official WhatsApp group for updates and communication. The PPT format will be shared in the WhatsApp group.
3
Immediately after joining the WhatsApp group
Preparing the PPT: Participants will work on their PowerPoint presentations based on the provided format.
4
13th Jan 2025
PPT Submission Deadline: All PPTs must be submitted by this date via the designated platform or email.
5
14th & 15th Jan 2025
Online Evaluation of PPTs & Presentations: Online evaluation of the PPTs will occur on these dates.
6
19th Jan 2025
Top Teams Shortlisted: The top teams will be shortlisted for the Grand Finale round.
7
10:00 AM
Event Start: The grand finale event will begin.

11:00 AM – 1:00 PM

1:00 PM – 3:00 PM

4:00 PM – 5:00 PM

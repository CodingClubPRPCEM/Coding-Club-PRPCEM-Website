Event Schedule

  • 1
    📅 19th Dec 2024 - 13th Jan 2025
    Registration Phase: Participants must complete their registration on the website by the end date.
  • 2
    📱 After 13th Jan 2025
    Joining the WhatsApp Group: Registered participants will be added to the official WhatsApp group for updates and communication. The PPT format will be shared in the WhatsApp group.
  • 3
    💻 Immediately after joining the WhatsApp group
    Preparing the PPT: Participants will work on their PowerPoint presentations based on the provided format.
  • 4
    📤 13th Jan 2025
    PPT Submission Deadline: All PPTs must be submitted by this date via the designated platform or email.
  • 5
    📝 14th & 15th Jan 2025
    Online Evaluation of PPTs & Presentations: Online evaluation of the PPTs will occur on these dates.
  • 6
    🏆 19th Jan 2025
    Top Teams Shortlisted: The top teams will be shortlisted for the Grand Finale round.
  • 7
    ⏰ 10:00 AM
    Event Start: The grand finale event will begin.
  • 8
    🎤 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM
    Inaugural Session: Official inauguration of the Techelons hackathon.
  • 9
    🔍 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM
    Round 1: Project Evaluation: Projects will be evaluated, and participants may be asked to add features or answer questions. Some participants may be eliminated based on project evaluation.
  • 10
    🏅 4:00 PM – 5:00 PM
    Final Round: Evaluation & Results: Final evaluation of shortlisted projects, followed by result announcements.
  • 11
    🎉 5:00 PM
    Event Conclusion: Hackathon concludes with the result announcement at the Unmesh 2025 ceremony.

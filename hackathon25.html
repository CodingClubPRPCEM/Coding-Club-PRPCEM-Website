Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

All students from engineering colleges can participate. Both individual and team participation is allowed.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and any development tools you prefer. Food and refreshments will be provided.

Is it inter-college and inter-department allowed?

Yes, students from different colleges and departments can participate and form teams together.

What are the team size requirements?

Teams can have 1-4 members. Individual participation is also welcome.

Is registration free?

Yes, registration is completely free for all participants.

What if the problem statements don't suit us?

You can propose your own innovative solution, but it should align with the hackathon's theme and objectives.

When will the PPT format be shared?

The PPT format will be shared on 1st January 2025 via email and on the official website.

When exactly is the hackathon happening?

The hackathon is scheduled for the third week of January 2026. Exact dates will be announced soon. Keep an eye on our updates!

Who can participate in this hackathon?

Students from all backgrounds and skill levels are welcome. Whether you're a beginner or experienced developer, there's a place for you.

Bring your laptop, chargers, and enthusiasm! We'll provide food, refreshments, and all the support you need during the event.

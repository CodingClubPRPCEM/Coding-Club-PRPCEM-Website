Our Journey

The Evolution of Innovation

From humble beginnings to becoming a recognized tech community, here's how we've grown and evolved over the years.

2020

Foundation & Vision

Coding Club PRPCEM was founded with a vision to create a platform for students passionate about technology and programming. Started with just 20 founding members.

20 Members First Workshop GitHub Organization
2021

First Major Milestone

Organized our first major hackathon "CodeFest 2021", attracting participants from multiple colleges and establishing our presence in the tech community.

100+ Participants ₹25K Prize Pool 5 Partner Companies

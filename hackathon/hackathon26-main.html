About Hackathon 26

Coding Club PRPCEM's Hackathon 26 challenges participants to build solutions for problems affecting their own college or community. It's an open-ended event: use any tech stack or approach.

Innovation First

Focus on creating innovative solutions that address real-world problems in your campus and community. Think outside the box and bring fresh perspectives.

Team Building

Work in teams of up to 4 members. Collaborate, learn, and build something amazing together. Perfect opportunity to network and make lasting connections.

Win Big

Compete for exciting prizes and recognition. Multiple categories ensure everyone has a chance to win. Show off your skills and get rewarded.

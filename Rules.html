Techelons 25

Hosted by P.R. Pote Patil College of Engineering & Management

Please note that rules are subject to change. The organizers have full authority to make modifications as necessary.

Timeline & Schedule

  • Registration: December 20 2024 - January 12 2025
  • PPT Preparation: January 1 2025 - January 12 2025
  • PPT Submission: January 12 2025
  • Presentation & Evaluation: January 13 2025 - January 14 2025
  • Grand Finale: January 20 2025

Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • All participants must register before the deadline.
  • Participants must be present for all rounds.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops.
  • Participants must adhere to the schedule.
  • Participants must follow the guidelines provided.
  • Teams can have a minimum of 3 members.
  • Teams can have a maximum of 5 members.
  • Participants must adhere to the code of conduct.

Submission Rules

  • All submissions must be made through the google form.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Ensure your project is well-documented.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation and creativity.
  • Technical complexity.
  • Presentation and clarity.

Join Us for an Exciting Journey!

Don't miss out on the opportunity to showcase your skills and win amazing prizes. Register now and be part of Techelons 25!

Event Information

The Hackathon will be conducted in 2 phases:

  • Phase 1: PPT evaluation round where participants get shortlisted for the finale.
    • Round 1: Online PPT presentation round.
  • Phase 2: Grand Finale round with 2 sub rounds
    • Round 2: Project evaluation round for prizes at PRPCEM Amravati.

Participants who get shortlisted for the finale round have to pay Rs. 250 as a Registration cost (using QR Code provided on website and flyer) for the final round.

Rules & Regulations

  • It is mandatory for all participants to join the WhatsApp group (link given on the Registration form) where instructions will be provided.
  • Any one member should be present at their table throughout the hackathons.
  • You have to work on the same idea and solution you submitted in phase 1.
  • You have to submit your code by 5:00 P.M. on 20th January 2024.
  • All code developed during the hackathon must be original work.
  • Seek help from mentors/organizers, but no cross-team collaboration is allowed.
  • Each team presents to the judging panel (demonstrating functionality, features, and innovation).
  • Submit code and project documentation before the deadline only.
  • Share code and projects with the community after the hackathon (open-sourcing encouraged).
  • Respectful and inclusive behavior is expected. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • Prizes for winning teams are based on judging criteria.
  • Organizers' and judges' decisions are final. Arbitration processes may apply for disagreements.
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules, format, or prizes (with clear communication).
  • You may be asked to consent to using your photos/videos/project details for promotion.
  • Three prizes are awarded (1st, 2nd, and 3rd) based on overall performance.
  • Team has to bring their own laptop, required software and equipment, only support power supply will be provided from organizers.
  • Evaluation time (1st – After 1 Hr. start of event and 2nd 1 Hr. Before end of event) is also included.
